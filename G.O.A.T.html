Who is the G.O.A.T in the NFL?

Tom Brady

Many argue that Tom Brady is one of the G.O.A.T's in the NFL.

  • Brady has won seven Super Bowls, which is the most by any quarterback.
  • Brady leads the league in passing yards, touchdowns, completions, attempts, and quarterback wins.
  • Brady played at a high level for a long time, while the average NFL career lasts only a few years.
  • Brady is known for his loyalty to teammates, helping teammates, and being a big brother to everyone.
  • Brady set the standard for aspiring quarterbacks and never experienced a drop in play.(Meaning just arriving and joining a game without booking a spot in advance)

Brady has alot to offer to the table on proving why he is the G.O.A.T of the NFL but, there is a young player who is rising to take this position.


Patrick Mahomes

People consider to add Patrick Mahomes into the race of being of the G.O.A.T's of the NFL. Is he?

  • Mahomes has won three Super Bowls and three Super Bowl MVP awards
  • Mahomes has the highest passer rating in playoff history at 105.8, and a playoff winning percentage of .833
  • Mahomes has appeared in the AFC Championship Game every season he's been the starter.
  • Mahomes holds the league records for touchdown passes (219) and passing yards (28,424) through 100 career regular-season games
  • Some say that Mahomes is reshaping the sport of football, and expanding the vision for what it can be.

While Tom is known have heavy champion records and stat records, but patrick improves with his abiltys every seaon leading him to soon take Tom's title. The call is close but who do you think is the G.O.A.T?
