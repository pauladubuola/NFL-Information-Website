The National Football League (NFL) was founded on September 17, 1920 in Canton, Ohio

The league's first 14 teams came from the Ohio League, Chicago Circuit, New York Pro Football League, and other lower midwest teams. The league's rules were based on college football for the first 12 years. In 1932, the NFL established its own Rules Committee to develop independent rule changes

There's been 52 super bowls since the start of the NFL.


American Football has been known to be one of the tuffest sports known to man... but it's also one of the most viewed sports in America.
