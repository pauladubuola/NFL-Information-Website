NFL Teams

There is about 32 teams registered in the NFL as of 2024 and they are slpit up between two conferences.


American Football Conference(AFC)

The American Football Conference is one of the two conferences of the NFL. It contains 16 teams with 4 divisions. (NFC West, NFC East, NFC South, and NFC North). The team with the most AFC Championships from the AFC is the Pittsburgh Steelers with 16 games.

National Football Conference (NFC)

The National Football Conference is the other one of the two conferences of the NFL. It's consisted of 16 teams organized into four divisions (NFC North, NFC South, NFC West, and NFC East). The team with the most NFC Championships from the NFC is the San Francisco 49ers with 19 wins.
